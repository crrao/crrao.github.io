Well, let me start with the proverbial "Hello World!" I suppose it is apropos that this blog and website begin during these COVID times. Time I would have spent toward something work related is now coming this way. Okay, that's a bit of a lie. The truth is this has been a project that has been on the backburner for quite a while, and I'm only now mustering up the wherewithal to give it a whirl. The idea of sharing my thoughts publicly, even if they are on matters of professional or technical interest devoid of personal details, seems like a rather scary proposition...which is exactly why I believe I must do it. I assure you this first post is more for me than it is for you, given I am popping my blogging cherry with every word I am typing and you are reading. I also assure you I won't have anything of substance for quite a while, so feel free to skip through the first several posts!
